what we do

Apple - You choose a Macbook, MacMini or an iPad and we set it up to be secure, backed up, connected to your iPhone and simple to use. Let Apple manage your contacts and your calendar so you can manage your business.

Google - Instant Messages, Email, Documents, Spreadsheets, Voicemail, we set them up so they can provide a collaborative suite of tools to run the foundation of your business. All of these Google tools are then hosted on your domain, you@yourbusiness.com giving a professinal look to your customer communications.

Website - Your storefront for the internet, the website needs to convey your sales pitch in a simple and attractive format. Websites should be easy to design, cheap to host and always available. Blog? We can help with that too. We use pre-designed templates from web design people that are great at what they do. With templates you wont be bombarded with questions about layouts and pixels, just point to the template you like and we do the rest.

how it works

1st meeting - We meet with you in a relaxed setting away from the office and learn. We learn what you want for your business on the internet. We find out what problems we can help solve using simple technology. We tell you about us and what we can offer. If you like what you hear we shake hands and start! By the end of the 1st meeting we will have a website template picked out and a rough outline of the content. We can also talk about what Apple combination you like.

2nd meeting - The website first draft is previewed and we make a list of changes that are needed. A list of employees is created to then add to Google Apps and Apple iCloud.

3rd meeting - The final product is launched at your business with a full day of side by side training and mentoring to get you productive.

Every month - You have one hour of support for anything we built; website updates, training, new employee onboarding or any questions you might have. If it will be more than an hour we will quote you a time to get the job done. This leaves you with assurance that your business will continue to grow with all the computer headaches gone or at least managed by us.
